Women's Day Celebration

यत्र नार्यस्तु पूज्यन्ते रमन्ते तत्र देवताः।

अर्थ:

जहाँ नारियों की पूजा होती है, वहाँ देवता निवास करते हैं।

Get ready as we take you through a series of exciting activities, each unfolding in a perfect sequence, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience for everyone!

  • To commence our event on an auspicious note, we begin with the sacred Lamp Lighting Ceremony , which will be performed by the esteemed women professors of our institution.
  • Up next, we have an inspiring speech by the distinguished students of our institution, showcasing their thoughts and insights.
  • Moving forward, our third activity presents a captivating showcase of poetry, eloquently delivered by our gifted students.
  • Next, we have a special moment of celebration as our distinguished female faculty members take part in the joyous Cake Cutting Ceremony.
  • Finally, we concluded our event with Gift Distribution Ceremony , where our esteemed Principal of GP Muzaffarpur honored our distinguished women educators.
