Women's Day Celebration

यत्र नार्यस्तु पूज्यन्ते रमन्ते तत्र देवताः।

अर्थ:

जहाँ नारियों की पूजा होती है, वहाँ देवता निवास करते हैं।

We took everyone through a series of exciting activities, each unfolding in a perfect sequence, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience for all!

  • We commenced our event on an auspicious note with the sacred Lamp Lighting Ceremony , performed by the esteemed women professors of our institution.
  • Next, we had inspiring speeches by the distinguished students of our institution, where they shared their thoughts and insights.
  • Our third activity featured a captivating poetry showcase, eloquently delivered by our gifted students.
  • Then, we had a special moment of celebration as our distinguished female faculty members took part in the joyous Cake Cutting Ceremony.
  • Finally, we concluded our event with a Gift Distribution Ceremony , where the esteemed Principal of GP Muzaffarpur honored our distinguished women educators.
